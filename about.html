My name is Timothy Judge. I was born in Southwestern Michigan into a large family and homeschooled until my high school graduation. Through homeschooling, my parents were able to offer me the one-on-one tutoring they thought education merited and helped to foster a strong work ethic. During my high school years, I also took classes from a local community college, Lake Michigan College, allowing me to get a headstart on some of my college work.

While I listed computer science on my application, my passion led me to switch to Scientific and Technical Communication before I arrived. I immediately became involved in the Douglass Houghton Hall Council, which helped to manage the residence hall I lived in. This helped me to develop my leadership skills in numerous ways.

My other involvements and interests include music composition, graphic design, web design, Supermileage Enterprise, soccer, and international travel. For more information about these interests, check out my projects.
